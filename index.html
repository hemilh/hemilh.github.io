GILLI DANDA

About Us

The Gilli Danda Foundation is a passionate initiative dedicated to providing children worldwide with a renewed opportunity at life. Through fundraising efforts, local and international events, and the invaluable contributions of our dedicated volunteers, we strive to make a meaningful impact on the lives of those in need, fostering hope and creating pathways to brighter futures.

MAKING A DIFFERENCE

At our children's nonprofit, we are committed to making a tangible and lasting difference in the lives of young ones across India. Through a multifaceted approach, we tirelessly strive to address the unique challenges faced by children in various regions. From providing essential education and vocational skills to street children in Mumbai, ensuring health and nutrition support in Jaipur's slum areas, to empowering orphaned youth in Chennai through skill development workshops, each initiative is meticulously designed to create positive, sustainable impact. Our "Safe Spaces for Girls" program in Kolkata and the eco-friendly school gardens project in Bangalore exemplify our dedication to fostering a nurturing environment that goes beyond immediate needs. Moreover, our sports for social inclusion initiative in Delhi stands testament to our belief in the transformative power of sports to instill essential life skills and promote unity. Through collaborative efforts and the generosity of our supporters, we endeavor to build a brighter future for every child, empowering them to break the cycle of poverty and reach their full potential. Join us in making a profound difference—one child at a time.
